About the Organization

Yuva Samiti Masdaha is a social and educational organization dedicated to promoting education in rural areas. Established in 2000, it focuses on empowering youth through knowledge and community service.

Mission

To provide quality education and opportunities to students in villages, fostering a spirit of competition and excellence.

History

Founded in 2000 by local educators, Yuva Samiti Masdaha has grown to serve thousands of students annually through its programs.

26 January Competition Test

Every year on 26 January, we conduct a competition test for students in Classes 5 to 10. It encourages academic excellence and celebrates Republic Day.

Date: 26 January | Venue: Masdaha School
